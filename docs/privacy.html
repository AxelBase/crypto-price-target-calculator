Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Crypto Price Target Calculator, a static, client-side web utility ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for this tool, which enables users to select cryptocurrencies, input prices, and compute required moves with volatility adjustments using live CoinGecko data. We prioritize user privacy through a design where all processing occurs in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This policy provides full transparency on our no-data-collection approach.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The tool operates without gathering any user data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: Names, emails, IP addresses, or device details.
  • Input Data: Selected cryptocurrencies, current/target prices, or calculated results remain local to your session.
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking of visits, interactions, or behavior via cookies, pixels, or third-party tools.
  • Session Data: The application is stateless; no persistent storage beyond temporary browser memory.

Since everything runs client-side, your inputs and results never leave your device.

3. How the Tool Functions

When using the Crypto Price Target Calculator:

  • Selections and Inputs: Crypto choices and price entries are processed locally using JavaScript.
  • Live Data Retrieval: Real-time prices and historical data are fetched directly from CoinGecko's public API in your browser—no intermediary servers involved.
  • Calculations and Visuals: Required moves, volatility adjustments, and charts are computed and rendered on your device only.

This architecture ensures complete isolation of your activity, ideal for private market analysis.

4. Third-Party Interactions

The only external calls are to CoinGecko's public endpoints for market data (CORS-enabled, no authentication required). These do not involve personal information. Chart.js is loaded via CDN but performs no tracking. No advertising, analytics, or other services are integrated.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, local storage for persistence, or any tracking technologies. Your experience is entirely anonymous.

6. Data Security

As no data is collected, security risks are minimal. We recommend using updated browsers and secure connections for API calls. Responsibility for device security lies with the user.

7. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly process any related data.

8. International Compliance

Our no-collection model aligns with global standards including GDPR and CCPA by design.

9. Policy Updates

Changes will be posted here with updated effective dates. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For questions, refer to the About page or tool repository.

We are committed to zero data collection, letting you analyze crypto targets privately.
